Our Speciality

Traditional Mats

Weaved straw or plantlike grasses called rushes, were the first form of mat found in Mesopotamia about 6,000 years ago. In biblical times, weaved mats were used by people with little money as sleeping pads to create a little bit of warmth from the cold earth floors of their homes.

Vintage Carpets

While any rug older than 80-100 years old is considered antique, the lesser-known range between antique rugs and vintage rugs 50-80 years old are labeled semi-antique. Any rug 20 years or older can be listed as vintage.

Designer Bed Sheets

Luxury sheets often have a percale or sateen weave. Percale is crisp and cool with a matte finish, while sateen is soft and smooth with a lustrous finish on one side. The weave can also affect the breathability and weight of the sheets.
